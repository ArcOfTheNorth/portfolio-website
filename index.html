Interests

As stated earlier, i have a serious passion in all things computer related (i.e software development, editing, artificial intelligence, game development, cybersecurity) Even though I have always been vaguely curious about how computers worked, I would say my interest was ignited by my Aunt Deborah when I was about 7 years old. She had come to visit for a few days and bought for me an arduino board. We spent the next few days working on projects, and from that point I was hooked.



I also have began to develop a strong interest in mathematics, and generally anything math related. This has not always been the case however, as I have spent most of my life either indifferent or strongly disliking math. In the past two years however, I have spent a lot of time stengthening my mathematical foundations, and have consequently found a lot more fun in the field. Additionally, i had a very good math teacher, who was able to show the fun that the subject could be. Because of this, I interact with a lot more math related content, and genuinely enjoy everything about it (my favorite creator is numberphile)

Finally, I am also very into volleyball, and have been playing in the school’s high school WAISAL (West African International Schools Activities League) team for the past two years. It is a sport that I find really fun, and I am highly fascinated by the teamwork that is required to play the game. Because of COVID, our team has not been able to travel and play against other schools in WAISAL, but we have hosted a few friendly games amongst our neighbourly schools.

Projects

Making an application for my school canteen


    With the help of two friends, I helped the company that provided food to my school by making them an application. The application itself digitized the logging process of food and drink orders. This kind of application not only helped reduce the ammount of paper content used in the school, but also made the recording process of orders more effecient.

Teaching children programming


    I have been teaching programming to younger students s a part time job for almost two years. The reason why I decided to venture into this field is because I saw that there was very little guidance by the teachers in my school in STEM, and I consequently made it my responsibility to help those who were curious learn a lot more.

Animation youTube channel

    A friend and I made an animation youTube channel, “Those animating dudes” In it we discussed relatable stories and funny experiences to try and bring joy to others. Our biggest video “Shower thoughts” features popular youTuber Tabbes.

Editing various videos

    Editing is something that I have always found really interesting. Over the years now, I have been editing a wide variety of videos, most of which featured my content. I believe it is something that I have significantly improved upon, and is something that I will continue to work on.
